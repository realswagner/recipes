Easy Beef Tacos!

Today we will make delicious easy tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 1 packed taco seasoning (any brand or spice level you like)
  • Hard or soft shell tortillas
  • Toppings: Cheese, tomatoes, Taco sauce, Lettuce, whatever else you like!

How to make:

  1. First heat pan to medium-high heat
  2. Add in 1lb lean ground beef
  3. Break the meat, cook and stir until browned
  4. Drain the fat and add beef back to pan
  5. add 1/2 cup water, taco seasoning, and simmer on low heat until water evaporates
  6. serve meat on tortillas with desired toppings
  7. enjoy!
