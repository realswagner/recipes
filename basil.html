Basil Chicken Pasta!


This recipe will yield a delicious basil chicken pasta.


Ingredients needed:


  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • store bought basil pesto sauce
  • seasoning for chicken: salt, pepper, garlic powder, italian herb blend
  • parmesan cheese
  • olive oil
  • 1lb penne pasta

How to make

  1. First, season chicken both sides and heat pan to medium with olive oil
  2. Sear both sides 3.5 minutes
  3. Put Chicken in over at 350 degrees F for 15 minutes
  4. Bring water to boil in medium pot and cook penne for 8 minutes while chicken cooks
  5. When pasta is drained and chicken finished, cut chicken and add to pasta
  6. Add parmesan and basil pesto sauce to chicken pasta
  7. enjoy!
