Taco dip recipe!

This will teach you how to make a delicious taco dip

Ingredients

  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 100g black olives
  • one can tomato paste
  • one onion (will only use half)
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 packet chili seasoning (chili powder is okay too)
  • 200g cream cheese
  • 100g shredded mixed cheese
  • 1 or 2 bags tortilla chips

How to make:

  1. First cook the beef until browned on medium high heat
  2. while meat is cooking, chop half an onion and your black olives
  3. once meat is cooked, drain the fat
  4. next add meat back to pan and stir in diced olives, tomatoes, tomato paste and onion. Also add a half packet of each chili and taco seasoning
  5. Add 200g cream cheese to bottom of a dish pan (I prefer glass)
  6. Once onions seem adequetely browned in the meat mixture, add to pan on top of spread cream cheese
  7. On top of the meat, cover meat and pan with mixed cheese
  8. put full pan in oven at 350 degrees F until cheese on top is melted and slightly browned
  9. serve portions with tortilla chips on side and enjoy!
